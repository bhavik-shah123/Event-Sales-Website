About This Events

CodeXchange Challenge'19

Codexchange is a confluence of potential key players in the capital market and budding developers. The event will have a Code-a-thon and a Virtual Trading Platform running simultaneously. Students will compete in teams, and work together to showcase their trading skills by investing in the right team in code-a-thon and competitive coding skills to solve the specific mind-boggling tasks. The winning team will be decided by calculating the cumulative score of the virtual stocks and code-a-thon performance by a particular team.


Our Mission !?

To start the ball rolling on investing in codes. We intend to create a real organisation like scenario where people can understand how the companies, stakeholders and developers work as a single unit.

What you will learn?

Master your trading skills, no matter your experience. Boost your problem-solving proficiency. Mechanics of organization. Diversify your network. Work collaboratively with other fellow developers and stockbrokers.

What are the benefits?

Experience the joy of real world competition. Gain insights of how an organisation works. Prize money worth ₹20,OOO. A certificate for attending the bootcamp. Cool schwags.

Got Questions?

CodeXchange Challenge'19

Can Traders code, or coders trade?
No, once you choose your role as a trader or a coder, you can’t change it.
Will all the teams compete on the final day?
No, there will be a preliminary test after the bootcamp session, based on which top 12 teams will compete on the final day.
What is Codexchange BootCamp?
Its a session on competitive coding and stock trading. You can attend it either online or offline. After the session, there will be an online preliminary test where we will select the top 12 teams for the final event.
When and how do we register?
Registrations will be done online. There also will be a registration desk, in AB-1 portico, VIT-AP University, Amaravati after 7th December, 2019.
How do you recover if your stocks are going down ?
If your team is not able to solve problems and is still in a loss, you can sell a part of your shares. Even then if your team is not able to recover from the loss and another team is ready to buy your company, the two teams will merge into one. But if you win the competition after this, the buyer is the winner.
Do we use virtual money for trading?
Yes, we use virtual money for trading.
Can there be fewer members in a team, or is it compulsory to have 6?
A team can have 4-6 members. Also, a team must be registered with 2 members from each sector i.e. CODING and TRADING. For the VIT-AP students, there is also an option of partial team registration.
Should we have background knowledge in trading?
No prior knowledge of trading is required. We will be training you for this in Bootcamp.
On what basis does the stock value change?
The stock value totally depends on the code-a-thon team’s performance.
What all programming language options do you have?
You will have the option of Java, c, c++ and python.
What level of problem statements will be given for coding?
The problem statements will be random, you will get problems of all levels.
My query is still answered, what do I do?
No worries. You can write your query to us at either gdsc.club@vitap.ac.in or bulls.club@vitap.ac.in. We will get in touch with you as early as possible.

Event Guideline

CodeXchange Challenge'19

The competition will completely be carried out on online platforms. Only top 15 performing teams in the Preliminary test will appear for the final day event. The preliminary round will be an online test consisting of questions based on coding logics as well as stock trading. Venues for the final day event will be different for both code-a-thon and stock trading. There will not be any sort of communication allowed between the coding squad and the trading squad. The questions for the code-a-thon will completely be randomised and stock price for a particular team will totally depend on its coding performnce. The winning will be decided on the cumulative scores of both code-a-thon and trading.
